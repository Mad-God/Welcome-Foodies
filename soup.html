Ingredients Qty
Oil 1 Tablespoon
Red Chilli 2
ginger 1 Teaspoon
Onion 1
vegetables 1 bowl chooped
Black pepper 1/4 Teaspoon
Vineager 6 Teaspoon
Noodles 1/3 cup
Oil For frying
  1. First chop all the veggies and keep aside.
  2. Heat oil in a wok or pan and add the chopped fresh red chilies, garlic and ginger. sauté for a minute. You can even add green chilies instead of fresh red chilies.
  3. Then add chopped onions. Sauté again for a minute.
  4. Now add finely chopped french beans and chopped mushrooms. stir fry on a medium to high flame till the mushrooms get lightly browned from the edges.
  5. Then add grated carrots, shredded cabbage, finely chopped capsicum (green bell pepper) & celery.
  6. Stir fry on a medium to high flame for 3 to 4 minutes. Add black pepper powder and soy sauce. Stir.
  7. Add water or veg stock to the stir-fried veggies.
  8. Season with salt. Add less salt initially as soy sauce already has salt in it.
  9. allow the soup to come to a simmer on medium flame.
  10. In the meantime, if you have cooked noodles, then add them to medium hot oil for frying. This is an optional step.
  11. Fry the noodles till they are crisp. Drain the fried noodles on kitchen paper towels.
  12. Meanwhile. Make a smooth paste of the corn starch with water.
  13. Add the corn starch paste to the soup.
  14. Stir very well and let the soup thicken.
  15. Lastly add rice vinegar or regular vinegar. Switch off the flame. Stir and check the taste of veg manchow soup. Add more seasonings like salt, black pepper or soy sauce or vinegar if required.
  16. Stir and Serve manchow soup hot topped with fried noodles or just plain garnished with coriander leaves (cilantro leaves).
